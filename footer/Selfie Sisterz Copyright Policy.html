    • Privacy and Identity Protection

    1.Consent for Photography:

    By participating in a photo session with Selfie Sisterz, you acknowledge and agree that your photograph will be taken. If you do not wish to have your photograph taken or used, please notify us before the session begins.

    2.Identity Protection:

    Selfie Sisterz understands the importance of privacy. If you wish to keep your identity hidden in the photographs used for promotional purposes, please inform us at the time of booking. We will make every effort to obscure or anonymize your identity in any images used publicly.
